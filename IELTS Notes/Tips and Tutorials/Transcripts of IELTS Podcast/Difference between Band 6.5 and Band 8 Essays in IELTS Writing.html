Difference between Band 6.5 and Band 8 Essays in IELTS Writing

August 21, 2023 By Ben Worthington

 

In this tutorial, we discuss the difference between band 6.5 and band 8 essays in IELTS Writing.

 

We look at:

  • How to transform a basic introduction into a sophisticated one.
  • Redundant words and how to replace them with academic expressions in IELTS.
  • How to use examples in your IELTS Essay to explain your ideas.
  • How to organize your paragraphs better in IELTS Writing.
